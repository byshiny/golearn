Locate Enrolment Center

You can get yourself enrolled anywhere in India. Locate nearby Aadhaar Enrolment Center of your convenience.

Enrolment Request (Online)

In case you are unable to find an enrolment centre near you, you can submit your Enrolment request here. UIDAI team will contact you. This service is available in some of the states.

Check Aadhaar Status

Already enrolled for Aadhaar, check if your Aadhaar is generated.

Download Aadhaar

Electronic version of Aadhaar

Get Aadhaar Number on Mobile

If your Aadhaar is generated, get Aadhaar number on your registered mobile by SMS.

Retrieve Lost UID/EID

If you have lost your Enrolment slip or Aadhaar number you can retrieve the same on your registered mobile.

Verify Aadhaar Number

Check if an Aadhaar number is valid or not deactivated.

Verify Email/Mobile Number

Check if your Email or mobile number is registered in Aadhaar database.

Address Update Request (Online)

Your Demographic details - Address is changed or modified. You can get them updated online.

Lock/Unlock Biometrics

Secure your Aadhaar Authentication as per your need by locking or unlocking your Biometrics.

Address Update Request (By Post)

In order to send your update request via post download the correction form.
View more details

Update at Permanent Enrolment Center

For both Demographic and Biometrics update you are required to visit the nearest Permanent Enrolment Centre(PEC).

Check Status - Updation done at Enrolment Center/ECMP

Check status of updation done at Enrolment Center by entering 28-digit Update Request number mentioned on Acknowledgement slip.

Check Aadhaar & Bank Account Linking Status

With this service you can check your Aadhaar and Bank account linking status.

Aadhaar Authentication History

With this service you can fetch the history of your up to 50 previous authentications.

Aadhaar Paperless Local e-KYC

It is a secure sharable document which can be used by any Aadhaar number holder for offline verification of Identification.
